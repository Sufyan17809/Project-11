Chevron Packing

With an aim to satisfy our clients, we are engaged in offering an extensive range of Chevron Packing. Das Seal

We are enlisted amongst the top names in the industry engaged in offering a quality approved range of Das Seal. Offered Das Seal is a double-acting seal and guide part comprising of a rubber seal, two split support rings and two split bearings. These are available in different sizes and are very reliable. Additionally, we also assure safe and timely delivery of our products.

Features:
  • Rigid construction
